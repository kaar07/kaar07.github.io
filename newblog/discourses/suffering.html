It is astounding how Nietzsche lived a miserable life and could still come up with something such as Ubermensch. After all, he did say that 'To live is to suffer, to survive is to find some meaning in the suffering.' The square-one unto which we shall be knocked out again and again otherwise, is to define suffering.

Suffering is an experience.
The association with pain (of all kinds) makes these experiences undesirable. Without experiences we cannot perceive the passage of time. So the question whether suffering (as in experience) is inevitable or not, does not make any sense! But when we associate suffering and pain it becomes something that we tend to avoid.
