Altruism

(The following is my opinion!)

Can Altruism exist without the existence of humans? Can it exist without anyone perceiving it?
Well, that depends on our distinction between co-operation and Altruism.

Cells exist and work in co-ordination with other cells. Living body mechanisms except for single celled organisms involve a phenomenal range of co-operation between cells. However, none of them are said to be altruistic.
I think it is safe to say that the distinction between co-operation and altruism is intent. Co-operation comes naturally when being born in into a regulated system. It might or might not serve altruistic purposes. However, altruism is explicitly about serving an altrusitc purpose with the intent of serving that purpose.
