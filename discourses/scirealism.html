Except humans no other species have any kind of interest in science. However, science can be used to explain to lot about each of them.
This is because science is only a model. It can be a very accurate model, in terms of prediciton of outcomes and other utilitarian aspects. Nonetheless, it is a still a model.

People often say that science deals with facts of the world. Well, in a way it is a true. But a better way of saying it would be science explains the facts of the world.
